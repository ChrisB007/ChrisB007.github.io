Rules

This is a single player game and the rules are pretty simple
 Your mission, if you choose to accept:
  • Provide the computer enough clues to guess correctly the image(s) you are shown in 30 seconds or less.
  • Your answers can be a "Yes" or "No".
  • You must not say the name of what's in the image.
  • If you must, you may use the "Clue box".
  • The computer scores point each time it fails to guess correctly.
  • You score points each time you help the computer guess the image(s) correctly.
